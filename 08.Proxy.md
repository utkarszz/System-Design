## PROXY ##
Youre deligating your request to someone else and then  the task is performed by someone else


## FORWARD PROXY ##
Assume clients are on a pvt server and instead of connecting to the internet they are connected to a proxy server, the proxy will block access to lets say social sites but will allow educational sites
## ADVANTAGES ##


## LOGGING ##
Allows you to log each req made by the user

## TRAFFIC CONTROL ##
You can govern what traffic to pass and which to block

## ENCRYPTION ##
Make sure that all traffic going through the proxy server is encrypted

## CLIENT ANONYMITY ##
