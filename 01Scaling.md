Scaling is a way to expand and accommodate more customers

## VERTICAL SCALING ##

Example: There's a book store. Initially few people came, later on the no of people coming increased but the area was constant making the whole place a mess so to prevent that the owner expanded the book store 
This is called  VERTICAL SCALING.

## HORIZONTAL SCALING ##

Example: There's a book store. Initially few people came, later on the no of people coming increased but the area was constant making the whole place a mess so to prevent that the owner opened its branches on multiple location so people can go to their preffered location.
This is called HORIZONTAL SCALING.
